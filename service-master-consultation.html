What this is, and what it isn't

You get everything we offer: the same TCM Wellness Report as our standard tier (the same questionnaire, the same photos of your tongue, face, hands, nails and ears), the personalised food therapy plan built on it, and a month of Food Therapy Coaching to put it into practice. The one difference is who reads your case. Our senior TCM doctors are excellent; this tier is read by the doctor they turn to.

It is insight and guidance, not medical treatment.

The report and plan are delivered digitally through your private channel, and the coaching runs in your own private group with our team. It does not include an appointment, a call, or any contact with the doctor herself. Any hands-on care is arranged separately with the clinic, in person. If you have a medical condition or concerning symptoms, please continue to see a licensed healthcare provider; this sits alongside that, it doesn't replace it.
