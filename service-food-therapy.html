Common questions

How is this different from just the report?

The TCM Wellness Report tells you your pattern. This adds a food plan built on it — exactly what to eat, cook and ease off for your body.

Do you sell or ship the ingredients?

No. We tell you what to look for and point you to trusted local Chinese grocers and herb shops near you. You pick it up yourself.

Is it a strict diet or meal plan?

No extreme protocols. It's practical guidance — foods to favour, simple recipes, things to ease off — matched to your pattern and easy to keep up.

Can I start with just the report and add this later?

You can — but the two are designed to go together, since the food plan only works once we understand your pattern.
