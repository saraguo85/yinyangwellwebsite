FAQ

Good questions, honest answers.

Everything you might want to know before you start. Can't find it? Message us and we'll answer.

Is this medical advice?

No. OneFlow offers lifestyle, dietary and wellness guidance based on Traditional Chinese Medicine. It isn't medical diagnosis or treatment and doesn't replace your GP or specialist. If you have a medical condition or worrying symptoms, please see a licensed healthcare provider — and never stop prescribed medication without your doctor.

Who actually reviews my report?

Every Constitution Report and food-therapy plan is read and signed off by a senior Traditional Chinese Medicine doctor at our partner clinic in Ningbo, China — doctors with decades of clinical experience. It's never auto-generated and left unchecked.

Do I need to be in the UK or China?

Neither. It's all remote — you fill in your intake online from anywhere, and your plan comes back to you digitally.

Why do you ask for tongue and face photos?

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, the tongue and complexion are two of the clearest windows into your internal pattern — warmth, damp, circulation. Clear photos in natural light let the doctor read your constitution far more accurately.

What's the difference between the £59 and £79 options?

The £59 Constitution Report tells you your pattern and what's driving it. The £79 option adds a personalised food-therapy plan on top — the specific foods, recipes and things to avoid for your body. Because a food plan only works once we understand your constitution, food therapy always includes the report.

How and when do I get my plan?

Once your intake is reviewed (usually within a few working days), your plan is delivered digitally and we'll set you up with a private channel — typically a WhatsApp thread or group — where you can read it and ask follow-up questions.

How does payment work?

Securely online via Stripe — card or the usual digital wallets. One-off services are paid upfront; the membership is a simple monthly or yearly subscription you can cancel anytime.

What is the Master Consultation?

A rare reading with one of the very few doctors who can diagnose by observation alone — seeing the root of an issue across body, mind and life. It's deep insight into why, not hands-on treatment. Availability is limited; enquire to arrange one.

Is Traditional Chinese Medicine safe alongside my normal healthcare?

The guidance we give is about food and lifestyle, designed to sit alongside conventional care, not replace it. Always keep your GP in the loop, keep taking prescribed medication, and tell us about any conditions so we can guide you sensibly.

Can I get a refund?

Because reports involve a doctor's time and are personalised to you, they can't be refunded once the review has started. If something's gone wrong, message us — we'll always try to make it right.

Still have a question?

Message us on WhatsApp and a real person (or, soon, our friendly assistant) will help.
