Common questions

What's a "solar term"?

The Chinese calendar divides the year into 24 short seasons — about a fortnight each. TCM has always adjusted how you eat and live with them; membership gives you a guide for each.

How often will I hear from you?

A seasonal guide every solar term (roughly fortnightly), weekly tips, and deep dives in between — never overwhelming.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, no questions. Monthly rolls month to month; annual saves you two months.

Is this medical advice?

No — it's seasonal lifestyle and food guidance from the TCM tradition. For a personalised read of your body, see the Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Wellness Report.
