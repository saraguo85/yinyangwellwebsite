Good to know

Common questions

Who actually provides the care?

Senior, licensed TCM doctors at our partner clinic in China. YinYang Well introduces you, coordinates the programme and translates — we don't diagnose or treat ourselves.

Do you prescribe or sell herbs?

No. If the doctors suggest herbs, you source them from your own trusted local shops. We don't sell or ship anything.

How often are the follow-ups?

It depends on the programme — monthly and quarterly options are available, each with regular check-ins. We'll set expectations clearly when you enquire.

Does this replace my GP?

No. It sits alongside your usual healthcare. Please keep seeing a licensed healthcare provider for any medical condition or concerning symptoms.
