What a Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Wellness Report is — and isn't

Western tests are brilliant at finding disease — something broken, out of range. They're not built to see how well your body is running when nothing is technically wrong. That's exactly the gap a constitution read fills: it looks at function, at the whole pattern, and turns a scatter of "random" symptoms into one story pointing in one direction you can work with.

It's not a diagnosis and it's not a cure — it's a clear map of how you're running right now.

It's read by senior, licensed doctors at our partner clinic in China; we coordinate and translate. Any herbs are something you'd source from your own local shops. If you have a medical condition or concerning symptoms, please see a licensed healthcare provider — a constitution read sits alongside that, it doesn't replace it.

Common questions

Do I need to be ill to get one?

Not at all. Plenty of people come simply wanting more energy, better sleep or steadier digestion. A read helps whether you're optimising or you're "off but fine on paper."

Is this a diagnosis?

No. It's a read of your constitution — insight into how your body is running and what supports it. It isn't medical diagnosis or treatment, and doesn't replace your GP.

What do you need from me?

A short online intake about your symptoms, sleep, digestion, energy and lifestyle, plus a clear photo of your tongue and face. Ten minutes, no account.

Who actually reads it?

A senior TCM doctor at our partner clinic in Ningbo, China — not an app, and not left unchecked. We coordinate and translate the write-up for you.
