Good to know

Common questions

Is this a diet or meal plan?

No. There are no strict rules or calorie counting. It's gentle, day-to-day guidance — small swaps, timing and cooking tweaks — matched to your pattern and easy to keep up.

Do I have to post every single meal?

Post what you can — the more you share, the more useful the feedback. Even a few meals a day gives us plenty to work with.

Is it private?

The group is small and private. If you'd rather not show your face or full name, that's completely fine.

What if I have a medical condition or take medication?

Please tell us at the start, and keep seeing your GP. This is food and lifestyle guidance to support how you feel — it isn't medical treatment and doesn't replace your care.

How is this different from the Food Therapy plan?

The Food Therapy plan is a one-off personalised plan. The Reset is a month of daily, hands-on coaching to actually put it into practice.
